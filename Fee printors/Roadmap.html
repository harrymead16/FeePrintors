Roadmap - Fee Printors

Fee Printors Roadmap

Our mission is to rebuild trust and grow our community while introducing valuable utilities for our NFT holders. This roadmap outlines our milestones and future plans for making Fee Printors a leading community on Solana.

Q1 - Rebuilding Trust & Community Growth

In the first quarter, our primary goal is to regain the trust of our community and rebuild our brand. We understand that a strong, engaged community is the foundation for long-term success, and we are committed to transparency, open communication, and regular updates. We will focus on the following tasks:

  • Rebranding Fee Printors with a fresh, community-driven vision.
  • Redesigning the website to enhance user experience and functionality.
  • Creating more engaging content to spark conversations within the community.
  • Building partnerships with other NFT projects to extend our reach and credibility.
  • Re-launching social media channels with a focus on delivering value.

This is a critical period where we focus on building the foundations for everything that will follow.

Q2 - NFT Utility Development

Q2 marks the beginning of developing real value for our NFT holders. We want to go beyond art and provide real-world utility to the Fee Printors collection. Here are some exciting initiatives:

  • Launching our first exclusive smart money insights platform, accessible only to NFT holders.
  • Introducing new educational content such as workshops, articles, and expert talks to empower our community.
  • Expanding partnerships with platforms and services to integrate Fee Printors into various DeFi ecosystems.
  • Introducing additional features for Fee Printors NFTs, such as staking and rewards mechanisms to drive holder participation.
  • Continuing the development of community-driven events like virtual meetups, Q&A sessions, and NFT giveaways.

Our goal in Q2 is to significantly increase the value of holding a Fee Printors NFT by offering exclusive content and utility features that benefit our community members.

Q3 - Expanding the Ecosystem

In Q3, we plan to expand Fee Printors’ ecosystem beyond just NFTs. We want to make sure that our project has a lasting impact on the broader Solana ecosystem. Our efforts will include:

  • Developing a set of innovative tools and platforms that help our community make smarter decisions in DeFi and NFTs.
  • Announcing exclusive partnerships with prominent platforms in the crypto and NFT space.
  • Hosting community events that will drive further adoption of Fee Printors NFTs, like hackathons or live collaboration projects.
  • Rolling out a community governance structure where NFT holders can actively participate in decision-making.
  • Building a reputation as a key player in the Solana NFT and DeFi ecosystem.

Q3 will be all about scaling the project, introducing new value-added features, and further integrating Fee Printors into the broader DeFi and NFT landscape.

Timeline

Q1: Rebranding & Relaunch

We will focus on rebuilding the community’s trust, relaunching the website, and rebranding Fee Printors.

Q2: Utility Launch

Our community members will begin to access NFT utilities like exclusive insights, workshops, and educational content.

Q3: Expanding the Ecosystem

Fee Printors will expand its ecosystem by introducing governance, partnerships, and additional value-driven features.
